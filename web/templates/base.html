AIP-BDET

AIP-BDET is a reliable BDE predictor based on the lightweight deep feed-forward networks and the layers of isolated substructure in organic molecules.

Its high performance came from the translation of the proper isolated substructure in organic molecules into the molecular fingerprints and the use of the lightweight deep feed-forward networks.

Our current model performance was tested on NVIDIA Quadro P1000 Laptop GPU with 9M+ parameters achieved 15k reactions per second (no featurization) or 600 to 800 reactions per second (with featurization), bringing the single-digit millisecond latency for each prediction.
